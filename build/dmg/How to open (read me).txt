Opening polyclaude for the first time
=====================================

polyclaude is not yet notarized by Apple, so the first time you open it
macOS may warn that it is "damaged and can't be opened". It is NOT damaged —
that message just means macOS is blocking an app it downloaded from the
internet (Gatekeeper).

To open it:

  1. Drag the polyclaude icon onto the Applications folder (the arrow on
     the right of this window).

  2. Open the Terminal app and paste this line, then press Return:

         xattr -cr /Applications/polyclaude.app

  3. Open polyclaude normally from your Applications folder.

You only have to do this once. After that it opens like any other app.
